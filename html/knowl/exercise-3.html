Hint

There are many ways to proceed, but putting A then B then C et cetera where they belong using swaps is one possibility. What swap puts A in the first position?

Solution

\begin{gather*} DCABFE \quad \mbox{(given) \phantom{swap D}} \\ ACDBFE \quad \mbox{swap D and A}\\ ABDCFE \quad \mbox{swap C and B}\\ ABCDFE \quad \mbox{swap D and C}\\ ABCDEF \quad \mbox{swap F and E} \end{gather*}
