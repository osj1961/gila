If \(\vec{v}\) and \(\vec{w}\) are two vectors of length \(m\) having components \begin{gather*} \vec{v} = \langle v_1, v_2, \ldots , v_m \rangle\\ \end{gather*}

and

\begin{gather*} \vec{w} = \langle w_1, w_2, \ldots , w_m \rangle \end{gather*} then we say \(\vec{v}\) and \(\vec{w}\) are equal and write \(\vec{v} = \vec{w}\) if and only if for every \(i\), \(1\leq i \leq m\), \(v_i = w_i\).
