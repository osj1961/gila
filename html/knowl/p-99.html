If \(\vec{v}\) is a vector having \(m\) components, \(\vec{v} = \langle v_1, v_2, \ldots , v_m \rangle\) and \(s\) is a scalar, then the scalar multiplication of \(\vec{v}\) by \(s\) is defined by \begin{equation*}s\vec{v} = s \langle v_1, v_2, \ldots , v_m \rangle = \langle sv_1, sv_2, \ldots , sv_m \rangle \end{equation*}
